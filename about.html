I think theres a huge responsibility on technology makers in building meaningful products, that will progress the evolution of mankind.

And I am ready to make my contribution!


What i do

I am always learning and striving to be better and to build meaningful products. I currently work with an incredible team at GAP as a developer, where we build business tools, that enable one of the largest fashion retail corporation in United States(Gap, Banana Republic, Old Navy, Athleta) to plan the lifecycle of their product inventory both in store and online.


Where I want to go

Though I come from a developer and a bit of creative backgrounds, I am now determined to transition into design, especially user experience research and design. Part of the reason is, even though we have amazing software products that solve hard problems , I often see users complaining how hard it is to use an application/software. When you build a product that is so cutting edge, it sure needs to be usable so that your users are not spending hours learning how to use it, rather acheiving great results while using it. This is where a user sees the real value and as a business we add a real reason to use the product.
